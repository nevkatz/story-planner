Story Length Estimator

This tool estimates story length using the MICE quotient, a simple formula based on the number of characters, scenes, and MICE threads in your narrative. (MICE stands for Milieu, Inquiry, Character, Event.)

Estimated length: —
L = ((C + S) × M × W) / 1.5
